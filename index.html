Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does therapy cost?

Individual sessions are £85 for 50 minutes. Couples sessions are £110. A free 20-minute consultation is available to all new clients before committing to any sessions.

Can I do sessions online?

Yes. We offer secure video sessions via an end-to-end encrypted platform. Online therapy is just as effective as in-person for most presentations, and many clients prefer the convenience.

How long will I need therapy?

This varies enormously by person and goal. Some clients find 6–8 sessions enough; others benefit from longer-term work. We'll regularly review your progress together so you're always in control.

Is everything I say kept confidential?

Absolutely. Everything shared in sessions is strictly confidential. The only exceptions are if there is a serious risk of harm to you or others — and even then, we would discuss this with you first wherever possible.

What if I've never tried therapy before?

That's completely fine — many clients come to us with no previous therapy experience. The free consultation is a no-pressure opportunity to ask any questions and see if it feels right for you.

Do you offer sliding-scale or reduced fees?

A small number of reduced-fee slots are available for clients on low incomes or in financial hardship. Please mention this during your consultation and we'll do our best to accommodate.
